📚 單字 Vocabulary
Q1–Q10
1 The word "ceasefire" in the news means ___. 單字
"The U.S. and Iran signed a preliminary MoU that established a 60-day ceasefire and a framework for reopening the Strait of Hormuz."
✅ 正確答案：A。Ceasefire（停火）指交戰各方同意暫停軍事行動，通常是臨時性的，不等於正式和平條約（peace treaty）。
💡 字根提示： cease（停止）+ fire（開火）→ 停止開火。
📝 例句：After months of fighting, both sides finally agreed to a ceasefire. （雙方終於在數月交戰後同意停火。）
2 Which of the following best defines "sanctions"? 單字
"The U.S. Treasury issued a 60-day sanctions waiver, allowing Iran to resume oil production and exports."
✅ 正確答案：B。Sanctions（制裁）是一國或國際組織對另一國採取的貿易、金融或外交限制，目的是迫使對方改變行為。
💡 記憶口訣： sanction 有「批准」和「制裁」兩個相反意思，在國際新聞中通常指 economic/trade sanctions（經濟制裁）。
📝 The U.S. lifted oil sanctions on Iran as a goodwill gesture.（美國解除對伊朗的石油制裁以示善意。）
3 The word "tenure" in the context of UK politics means ___. 單字
"His departure ends a turbulent tenure marked by mounting pressure from within his own party."
✅ 正確答案：C。Tenure（任期）指某人擔任某職位的時間段。在英國政治中，Starmer's tenure as PM 指他擔任首相的這段時期。
💡 字根提示： ten- 來自拉丁文 tenere（持有）→ tenure = 持有職位的時間。
📝 His tenure as CEO lasted for five years.（他擔任執行長的任期長達五年。）
4 Choose the best meaning for "frontrunner". 單字
"Andy Burnham is widely considered the frontrunner to become Britain's next prime minister."
✅ 正確答案：C。Frontrunner（最具優勢的候選人）指在競賽或選舉中目前領先、最可能獲勝的人。
💡 字面拆解： front（前面）+ runner（跑者）→ 跑在最前面的人 → 領先者。
📝 She emerged as the frontrunner for the position after her impressive performance.（她在出色表現後成為最具優勢的候選人。）
5 The adjective "unprecedented" means ___. 單字
"The 2026 FIFA World Cup is the largest in the tournament's history, featuring an unprecedented 48 national teams."
✅ 正確答案：B。Unprecedented（史無前例的）指從未發生過的事情。
💡 字首解析： un-（否定）+ precedent（先例）+ -ed → 沒有先例的 → 史無前例的。
📝 The heatwave reached unprecedented temperatures across Europe.（熱浪使整個歐洲達到史無前例的高溫。）
6 What does the verb "retaliate" mean? 單字
"In a tit-for-tat response, China moved to retaliate against U.S. sanctions on Chinese tech companies."
✅ 正確答案：C。Retaliate（報復）指以相應或類似手段回應對方的不友善行為。
💡 同義詞： get back at, strike back, hit back。搭配常用 retaliate against/for。
📝 The company retaliated by raising tariffs on imported goods.（該公司以提高進口商品關稅的方式報復。）
7 The term "dual-use" in the tech sanctions story refers to ___. 單字
"Chinese companies would be blocked from exporting 'dual-use' items — goods that have both civilian and military applications."
✅ 正確答案：B。Dual-use（軍民兩用）指同時具有和平（民用）與軍事用途的技術或產品，如無人機、高效能晶片等，因此受到出口管制。
💡 記憶口訣： dual = 兩個，use = 用途 → 兩種用途 → 軍民兩用。
📝 Many semiconductor technologies are considered dual-use and subject to export controls.（許多半導體技術被視為軍民兩用，受出口管制約束。）
8 The verb "escalate" most closely means ___. 單字
"Tensions between the United States and China over technology and national security escalated further this week."
✅ 正確答案：C。Escalate（升級）指情況、衝突或緊張程度逐步加劇。
💡 字根提示： scala（拉丁文，梯子）→ escalate = 像爬梯子一樣往上升 → 升級。反義詞：de-escalate（降低緊張）。
📝 The dispute escalated into a full diplomatic crisis.（這場爭端升級為全面的外交危機。）
9 Which sentence uses "persist" correctly? 單字
"The extreme heat, which scientists link to climate change, is expected to persist through at least the end of the week."
✅ 正確答案：A。Persist（持續；堅持）是不及物動詞，後面不直接接受詞，表示某狀況持續存在或某人堅持做某事。
💡 常見搭配： The problem persists. / She persisted in her efforts. / Symptoms may persist for days.
❌ 選項B、C、D錯誤，因為 persist 後面不能直接加受詞名詞。
10 The adjective "diplomatic" best describes ___. 單字
"Vice President Vance met with Iranian representatives, signaling the highest-level direct diplomatic contact between the two countries in decades."
✅ 正確答案：B。Diplomatic（外交的）指與國家間官方交涉、談判或關係相關的。名詞形式：diplomacy（外交）；執行者：diplomat（外交官）。
💡 補充： diplomatic 也可以用來形容一個人處事圓融、說話得體：She was very diplomatic in handling the conflict.（她在處理衝突時非常圓融。）
📖 文法 Grammar
Q11–Q20
11 Choose the correct form: "U.S. and Iranian negotiators ___ a preliminary agreement on June 15." 文法
新聞句：The U.S. and Iran appear to be moving toward a historic diplomatic agreement.
✅ 正確答案：B。句子中有明確的過去時間點「on June 15」，應使用簡單過去式（Simple Past）。
💡 口訣： 有明確時間點（yesterday, last week, in 2020, on June 15）→ 用過去式；沒有時間點或強調「已完成且影響現在」→ 用現在完成式。
❌ A（have signed）適合說「近期已完成，影響現在」但不搭配具體過去時間點。
12 Which sentence is in the passive voice? 文法
新聞句：China announced sanctions on ten American military-related companies.
✅ 正確答案：B。被動語態（Passive Voice）結構：主詞 + be動詞 + 過去分詞 (+ by + 施動者)。
💡 口訣： 主動 → 「誰」做了什麼；被動 → 「什麼」被誰做了。新聞中被動語態很常見，用來強調被影響的對象。
📝 被動：The agreement was signed by both parties.（協議由雙方簽署。）主動：Both parties signed the agreement.
13 Identify the relative clause in the sentence below: 文法
新聞句："Andy Burnham, who won the Makerfield by-election convincingly, is widely considered the frontrunner to become Britain's next prime minister."
✅ 正確答案：C。關係子句（Relative Clause）以關係代名詞（who, which, that）開頭，修飾前面的名詞。
💡 結構： [先行詞] + [關係代名詞 + 子句]。這裡 "who" 代替 Andy Burnham，子句 "who won the Makerfield by-election convincingly" 是補充說明。
📝 前後加逗號的關係子句是「非限定用法」（non-defining），提供額外資訊，不影響主句意思。
14 Which sentence uses indirect speech (間接引語) correctly? 文法
原始直接引語：VP Vance said, "Iran will allow nuclear inspectors back into the country."
✅ 正確答案：B。將直接引語改為間接引語（reported speech）時，若主句動詞為過去式（stated），子句中的動詞需「後移一個時態」：will → would。
💡 時態後移口訣：
will → would | can → could | is/are → was/were | have → had
📝 She said, "I am happy." → She said that she was happy.
15 Choose the correct conditional sentence (條件句): 文法
新聞脈絡：Analysts say a final peace deal between the U.S. and Iran is still uncertain.
✅ 正確答案：C。第一條件句（First Conditional）結構：If + 現在簡單式，主句 + will/can + 原形動詞。表示真實或可能發生的情況。
💡 三種條件句口訣：
第一型（真實可能）：If + V現在，will + V
第二型（不真實假設）：If + V過去，would + V
第三型（與過去相反）：If + had + pp，would have + pp
16 What is the function of "However" in the sentence below? 文法
"The U.S. and Iran have reached a preliminary agreement. However, significant obstacles remain before a final peace deal can be signed."
✅ 正確答案：B。However（然而）是轉折副詞，用來引出與前句相反或對比的資訊，表示「雖然……但是……」。
💡 常見轉折詞： however, nevertheless, nonetheless, yet, on the other hand, despite this
💡 常見原因詞： because, since, as / 結果詞： therefore, thus, as a result / 補充詞： moreover, furthermore, in addition
17 Identify the appositive phrase (同位語) in the sentence: 文法
"Andy Burnham, the Mayor of Greater Manchester, won the Makerfield by-election and is now the frontrunner for Labour leader."
✅ 正確答案：B。同位語（Appositive）緊接在名詞後面，以逗號隔開，對該名詞補充說明或重新界定。
💡 結構： [主詞名詞], [同位語（對主詞的補充說明）], [動詞...]
📝 Taiwan, an island nation in East Asia, is known for its semiconductor industry.（台灣，東亞的一個島國，以半導體產業聞名。）
📝 考試中同位語常被用來測試逗號的使用與句子結構的理解。
18 Choose the sentence with the correct participial phrase (分詞構句): 文法
新聞脈絡：Starmer faced mounting pressure, and he announced his resignation.
✅ 正確答案：B。分詞構句用現在分詞（V-ing）或過去分詞（V-ed）取代副詞子句，主詞要與主句相同。
💡 結構： [V-ing/V-ed + ...], [主句]。
A 錯誤：多餘的 "he"。C 錯誤：主句時態不一致（announces應為announced）。D 錯誤：語序混亂。
📝 Knowing the risks, she still chose to proceed.（明知風險，她仍選擇繼續。）
19 Which sentence correctly uses "not only ... but also"? 文法
新聞脈絡：The France heat wave is dangerous and also unprecedented in its intensity.
✅ 正確答案：A。Not only ... but also ... 的標準結構要求兩個相連部分的語法結構對稱（平行結構 parallel structure）。
💡 平行結構口訣： not only [形容詞] but also [形容詞]；not only [動詞] but also [動詞]。
📝 She is not only intelligent but also hardworking.（她不僅聰明，而且勤奮。）
❌ C 錯誤：not only 位於句首時，主詞與動詞需要倒裝：Not only is the heat wave dangerous, but it is also unprecedented.
20 What is the function of "to ensure" in the sentence below? 文法
"Starmer set out a timetable with nominations opening on July 9 to ensure a new leader is in place before Parliament returns in September."
✅ 正確答案：B。這裡的 "to ensure" 是目的不定詞，說明 Starmer 制定時間表的「目的」——為了確保新黨魁在議會返回前就位。
💡 目的不定詞口訣： 當 to + V 可以用「為了……」解釋時，就是目的不定詞。也可用 in order to 替換，意思相同。
📝 She studied hard to pass the exam.（她努力學習，目的是通過考試。）
= She studied hard in order to pass the exam.
